Spatial Notter Privacy Policy

Spatial Notter is a mobile application that allows users to take notes based on their spatial location and visualize them on a map. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information from and about you when you use our app. Information We Collect When you use Spatial Notter, we may collect information about you, including: -Personal information, such as your name and email address, when you create an account. -Location information, including the location where you take notes and your device's location. -Note content, including any text, images, or attachments that you include in your notes. This information is never transmitted to any server related to us and is used for your personal experience. How We Use Your Information We use the information we collect to provide and improve the app, including: Storing and displaying your notes based on spatial location. Personalizing your experience with the app, such as by suggesting nearby points of interest or providing location-based reminders. How We Share Your Information We do not share your information as this data is stored locally unless when automatticaly backed up to your google account. Data Retention We do not retain your information. Security Wen do not collect or transmit any information out of your app or phone Children's Privacy Spatial Notter is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
